Terms & Policies

Information about our booking terms, deposit policy and refunds.

Booking Policy

To secure your appointment, a 20% deposit is required at the time of booking. The remaining balance is payable on arrival at the clinic. Deposits are applied towards your treatment cost.

We kindly ask for at least 48 hours’ notice if you need to reschedule your appointment. Changes made with less than 48 hours’ notice may result in the loss of your deposit.

Deposits & Refunds

Deposits are non‑refundable. If you miss your appointment without notification or cancel at the last minute, the deposit will not be returned. If you reschedule with at least 48 hours’ notice, your deposit may be transferred to a new appointment.

Treatments and products purchased are non‑refundable once received. Results vary from person to person. We strive to provide you with a clear treatment plan and realistic expectations during your consultation.

Consultations

Consultations are offered to assess suitability for treatment. It is your responsibility to disclose any relevant medical history, allergies or medications. We reserve the right to refuse treatment if it is deemed unsuitable for your health or well‑being.

Privacy

Your personal information is kept confidential and used solely for booking and communication purposes. We do not share your data with third parties without your consent.
